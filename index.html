Corgis & HTML Basics

What are Corgis and how are they related to HTML?

Corgis are majestic dwarf dogs with fluffy coats, big ears, chunky thighs, unending sass, and an abundence of adorableness! Corgis relate to HTML in few to no ways, but they sure are fun to look at!


Corgi Traits:

Clever
Bred to herd, corgis are clever and love to have jobs around the house.
Agile
Despite their small size, corgis are very athletic and surprisingly quick on their feet.
Lively
Nothing stops these shorties, corgis are independent go-getters with HUGE personalities.
Affectionate
Corgis take their duty of companionship very seriously. They are known to be very attentive cuddle-bugs.
Goofy
With their big personalities and little chunky legs, it's a challenge to get through the day without a good chuckle.

    4 Corgi Fun Facts:

  1. The breed has been around since 1100 CE.
  2. Welsh legend describes corgis as a gift from the fairies. Some legends saying they were battle companions, carriage pullers, and herders for the fairies originally.
  3. In Welsh, corgi means dwarf dog.
  4. The scruff on their neck is sometimes referred to as a "fairy saddle".
    Corgi Stats:
  • 10-12 inches tall
  • 28-30 pounds
  • 12-13 year lifespan
  • Variety of colors and markings.
